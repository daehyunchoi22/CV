About Me

My research interest is to study the interaction between liquid-gas / fluid-solid by experiments together with theoretical approaches as well as numerical tools. Using optimzied experimental design and multidisciplinary collaboration, I have contributed a variety of research fields such as spray process, droplet production, flow measurement technique, biology, collective behavior, surgical devices, disease transmission, digital twin as well as fundamental fluid mechanics.

  • Go to Research Areas

I am a postdoctor at Ajou University working with Professor Daeshik Kang. I earned my Ph.D. degree in the department of mechanical engineering at Seoul National University in 2022 under the supervision of professor Hyungmin Park. At the same department, I recieved M.S., and B.S. degrees in 2018, and 2015, respectively. I recieved the best Ph.D. thesis award at Seoul National Univerity. Currently, I am seeking my postdoctoral position!

Research Areas

  • Liquid-gas Interaction

    Understanding dominating interface dynamics during mass/momentum transfer between phases using precise measurements.

  • Fluid-solid Interaction

    Elucidating the coupling dynamics between solid and fluid when large amout of momentum is exchanged between them.
